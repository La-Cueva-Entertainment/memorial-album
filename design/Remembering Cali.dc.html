about Cali

Cali was the funny one — the sister who could turn a boba run into an adventure and a mahjong table into a comedy show. She loved fiercely: her family, her friends, her cats, and the messy, sun-soaked city of Los Angeles she called home.

She had a nickname for everyone, a soft spot for good food and better company, and a way of making whoever she was with feel like the most important person in the room.

She gave so much more than she ever asked for. This little place is for all of us — to keep her close, share the photos and the stories and the laughs, and look after each other the way she always looked after us.
